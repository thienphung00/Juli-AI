## 4. Content Obligations

### 4.1 Community Guidelines Compliance

E-commerce content must comply with TikTok Community Guidelines. Content violations
accumulate Creator Health Rating (CHR) deductions, including Community Guidelines violations
detected in LIVE sessions.

### 4.2 Prohibited Practices (known, non-exhaustive)

| Practice | Status |
|---------|--------|
| Making false health / safety claims about promoted products | Prohibited (misinformation policy) |
| Promoting products from prohibited / restricted categories | Prohibited |
| Reproduced / duplicated content | Prohibited (48 VP for sellers; creator-equivalent exists in CHR) |
| Affiliate link sharing without complying with disclosure rules | Prohibited (FTC/CMA-style; VN-specific requirements UNKNOWN) |

Full prohibited content article: UNKNOWN — not retrieved in this pass. See
https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/home?role=creator&menu=policy → "Nội dung bị hạn chế & bị cấm"

### 4.3 Advertising Disclosure (UNKNOWN for VN)

FTC/CMA-style affiliate disclosure obligations are standard in US/UK markets. VN-specific
disclosure requirements are **UNKNOWN** in this pass. Mark as `REGION-VARIANT` until
verified against VN-specific policy article.

## 6. Creator Health Rating (CHR) — Compliance Framework

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| CHR scale | 0 – 1,000 |
| New creator baseline | 200 points |
| Deduction trigger | Violation of Content Policy, Creator Terms, Community Guidelines (LIVE) |
| Point deduction reset | Automatic after 90 days |
| Milestone enforcement | At CHR 150, 100, 50, 0 (see [policy.md](policy.md)) |
| Loss of e-commerce permissions | CHR = 0 |
| Severe cases | Platform may remove e-commerce permissions outside CHR framework (repeated/severe misconduct) |

Source: https://seller-us.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=5054301796321070&lang=en  
REGION-VARIANT: VN thresholds may differ — verify against VN Policy Center.
