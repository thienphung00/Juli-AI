## Section 6: Account Status — Creator Health Rating (CHR)

### CHR Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| System | Creator Health Rating (CHR) — replacing older violation-points model |
| Scale | 0 – 1,000 points |
| Starting points | 200 (all new TikTok Shop creators) |
| Points lost for | Violations of Content Policy, Creator Terms of Use, Community Guidelines (in LIVE sessions) |
| Points gained for | Quality content, quality LIVE sessions, successful orders, passing policy quizzes |
| Reset cadence | Point deductions reset automatically after 90 days |
| View location | Creator Center → Account Health → Creator Health Rating |
| Unified view | Shows both TikTok Shop violations AND general TikTok Community Guidelines violations from LIVE |
| Source | https://seller-us.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=5054301796321070&lang=en |

**Note:** The US (seller-us.tiktok.com) article was retrieved; VN-specific CHR details may
vary. Mark all CHR thresholds as `REGION-VARIANT: may differ for VN`.

### CHR Zones and Milestone Enforcement

| Zone | CHR Range | Status | Enforcement |
|------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Green (Healthy) | 200 – 1,000 | Healthy | None |
| Orange (Needs attention) | 151 – 199 | At risk | Warnings issued |
| Red (Unhealthy) | 1 – 150 | Unhealthy | Milestone enforcement applies |
| Black (Deactivated) | 0 | Deactivated | E-commerce permissions removed |

### Milestone Enforcement Thresholds

Enforcement actions are triggered automatically when CHR drops to:
- **150** — first milestone enforcement
- **100** — second milestone enforcement
- **50** — third milestone enforcement
- **0** — permissions removed / account effectively deactivated for e-commerce

Warning notifications are sent when score approaches the next milestone.

Source: https://seller-us.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=5054301796321070&lang=en

### CHR vs Violation Points (Legacy)

| Dimension | Old system (Violation Points) | New system (CHR) |
|-----------|------------------------------|-----------------|
| Scale | Points accumulate upward (bad) | Score degrades downward (bad) |
| Starting state | 0 VP | 200 CHR |
| Enforcement | Thresholds at 12, 24 VP | Thresholds at 150, 100, 50, 0 |
| E-commerce revocation | At 24 VP | At 0 CHR |
| VN launch | Pre-2026 | Phased in 2026 (same timeline as seller AHR) |

**DISCREPANCY:** The creator-side CHR article was found on seller-us.tiktok.com (US site).
The VN Policy Center navigation shows "Tình trạng tài khoản" (Account Status) section exists,
but the specific CHR thresholds for VN are UNKNOWN — may match US thresholds or differ.
Cross-check against https://seller-vn.tiktok.com when VN-specific article is available.

### Violation Records Access

Creators can view:
- Exact points added or removed per violation
- Reasons for each violation
- Any enforcement actions taken
- Suggestions to avoid future violations
- Option to appeal if decision was made in error
