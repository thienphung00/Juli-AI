## 1. System Transition: Violation Points → Account Health Rating

TikTok Shop is replacing the Violation Points (VP) system with an **Account Health Rating (AHR)**:

| Milestone | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| May 2026 | AHR preview launched — visible in Seller Center, reference only; enforcement still on VP |
| Mid-June 2026 | AHR begins gradual replacement of VP |
| July 2026 | AHR fully replaces VP system |

**REGION-VARIANT: VN** — The transition timeline was announced on seller-vn.tiktok.com.

## 2. Violation Points System (Legacy — active through June/July 2026)

### 2.1 How VP Works

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Assignment | Points issued per policy violation; severity determines point count |
| Reset | Auto-reset every 90 calendar days — **unless** seller has accumulated 48 VP (permanent suspension) |
| Visibility | "Shop Health" page in Seller Center → Violation Records tab |
| Warnings | Sent ~5 VP before each milestone threshold |

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=10015960&lang=en

### 2.2 VP Milestone Enforcement

| Milestone | Enforcement Actions |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **12 VP** | Cannot enroll in new campaigns, user growth, free shipping subsidies for **7 days**; cannot enroll in Affiliates Program for 7 days |
| **24 VP** | Cannot enroll in mega campaigns, user growth, free shipping for **14 days**; no Affiliates enrollment for 14 days; **listing creation suspended 14 days**; **LIVE traffic reduced 14 days**; **Shopping Center recommendations removed 14 days** |
| **36 VP** | Shop **deactivated 28 days**; no mega campaigns / user growth / platform benefits for **60 days** |
| **48 VP** | **Permanent suspension** + potential legal action + referral to regulatory authorities |

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=10015960&lang=en

## 3. Account Health Rating System (AHR — replacing VP from July 2026)

### 3.1 How AHR Works

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Scale | 0 – 1,000 (higher = healthier) |
| New seller starting score | 200 points |
| Points earned | Successfully fulfilled orders (weekly calculation); passing policy quizzes |
| Points deducted | Policy violations; failing performance standards |
| Calculation window | Last 90 days (some markets: 180 days — REGION-VARIANT) |
| Formula | `AHR = 200 (base) + Points earned (orders, last 90d) − Points deducted (violations, last 90d)` |
| Update cadence | Regularly; order points calculated and added weekly |

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8831988245645057&lang=en  
Source: https://seller-id.tokopedia.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=8199624385775361 (Indonesia — for reference)

### 3.2 AHR Zones

| Zone | AHR Range | Status | Enforcement |
|------|----------|--------|-------------|
| Green | 200 – 1,000 | Healthy | None |
| Orange | 151 – 199 | Needs improvement | At risk of milestone enforcement |
| Red | 1 – 150 | High risk | Milestone enforcement applies |
| Black | 0 | Deactivated | Shop deactivated |

**REGION-VARIANT: VN vs US** — US AHR defines orange zone as 51–199, red zone as ≤50.
VN AHR zones confirmed as above (200–1000 Green, 151–199 Orange, 1–150 Red, 0 Black) based
on announcement article (seller-vn.tiktok.com).

### 3.3 AHR Milestone Enforcement Thresholds

| AHR Score | Enforcement |
|-----------|------------|
| 150 | First milestone enforcement |
| 100 | Second milestone enforcement |
| 50 | Third milestone enforcement |
| 0 | Shop deactivated |

Enforcement warnings sent when AHR is 10 points away from next milestone.

Source: https://seller-us.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=6750828276418350&lang=en

## 4. Enforcement Actions (General)

In addition to milestone-specific actions, TikTok Shop may take:

- Formal warning messages
- Suspension of listing privileges / product delisting
- Traffic suppression (ads, LIVE, videos, listed products)
- Removal from Shopping Center recommendations
- Order / listing volume caps
- Temporary or permanent withdrawal of seller benefits
- Suspension from Affiliates enrollment and campaigns
- Temporary or permanent account suspension
- Balance withholding (see §5)
- Cancellation of promotions / subsidies
- Account deletion
- Legal action / referral to regulatory authorities
- Extended settlement period
- Removing seller's ability to approve customer refunds (TikTok handles directly)

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=10015235&lang=en

## 5. Balance Withholding

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Trigger | Reasonable belief of policy violation (at Platform's sole discretion) |
| Scope | Any monies due or becoming due, including sales proceeds |
| Duration | Longer of: 180 calendar days from notification, OR completion of investigation |
| Compensation | If breach caused loss to TikTok/buyers/third parties, seller may be required to pay from withheld balance |
| Remaining balance | Returned to seller after compensation paid |
| Notice | Platform will notify seller when restriction is placed |

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=10015235&lang=en

## 6. Appeals

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Max appeals | 2 per enforcement action |
| First appeal deadline | Within 30 days of enforcement notification |
| Second appeal deadline | Within 15 days of first appeal rejection |
| Late appeals | Not accepted |
| Outcome | Not guaranteed; Platform evaluates evidence at sole discretion |
| Correction alternative | Available for some violations (12–72 hour window depending on violation type) |
| Where | Seller Center → Shop Health page → Violation Records → appeal/correct |

## 7. Associated Account Enforcement

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Definition | Accounts with sufficient connections (e.g. same login) to indicate shared ownership |
| Consequence | Violations on associated accounts may trigger enforcement on the primary seller account |
| Scope | Can include other seller accounts, creator accounts, or other TikTok accounts |
