## Payment Settlement Periods

**Effective June 9, 2026** — Four-tier framework replaces the prior three-tier system.

| Tier | Period | Eligibility |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Express** | 1 calendar day | TikTok Shop Mall + Store Rating ≥ 4.5, OR qualified Star Shop+ |
| **Accelerated** | 3 calendar days | Shops that have passed the New Shop Adjustment Period |
| **Standard** | 8 calendar days | Shops that have NOT yet passed the New Shop Adjustment Period |
| **Extended** | 15 calendar days | Applied based on account risk, activity evaluation, or policy violations |
| **Maximum** | 30 calendar days | In cases of abnormal or potentially harmful activity |

**Prior system (before Jun 9, 2026):**
- Express: 3 days
- Standard: 7 days (default for non-Mall)
- Extended: 15 days (default for Mall, or elevated risk)

**Settlement periods are dynamic** — assessed on an ongoing basis as shop performance evolves.

**Creator commissions:** Paid out after seller settlement; payout timelines for creators
reflect the seller's settlement tier.

Source: May 2026 update — policy URL

## Affiliate Collaboration Qualification — SFRR Thresholds

These thresholds apply to **individual products** with ≥5 seller-fault returns.

| Category | SFRR threshold |
|----------|---------------|
| Electronics | < 3% |
| Lifestyle & Fashion | < 2.8% |
| FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods) | < 2.5% |

**Shop-level gates also apply** — see [programs-and-eligibility.md](programs-and-eligibility.md).

## Negative Review Rate (NRR) — Affiliate Gate (from Apr 2026)

- Service-related NRR violations disqualify a shop from Affiliate Marketing
- Service-related NRR = % of service-related negative reviews out of all delivered orders in last 30 days
- Category-based NRR thresholds for affiliate product eligibility (historical, may be updated):
  - Electronics, Fashion: < 1.5% (from Sep 2024)
  - Home & Living, Health, Beauty & Personal Care: < 1.3% (from Sep 2024)
  - Groceries, Mom & Babies: < 1% (from Sep 2024)
  - Note: current thresholds after Apr 2026 updates — service NRR is a separate disqualifier

Source: Sep 2024 and Mar/Apr 2026 updates

## Fulfillment SLA Thresholds

| Metric | Threshold | Effect of breach |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|
| Fast Dispatch Rate (FDR) | High (UNKNOWN exact %) | Affects Store Rating; fewer next-day delivery labels |
| Late Dispatch Rate (Standard) | Low (UNKNOWN exact %) | Affects Store Rating |
| Instant Late Dispatch Rate | < 5% (7-day rolling) | Service restrictions if exceeded AND ≥5 qualifying orders |
| Same-day Late Dispatch Rate | < 5% (7-day rolling) | Service restrictions if exceeded AND ≥5 qualifying orders |
| After-Sales Handling Time (AHT) | < 20 hours | Store Rating impact |

**Business hours setting:** Must be accurately configured in Seller Center → Shipping
Settings to avoid avoidable Instant/Same-day LDR violations.

**FDR/LDR cutoff times (effective Dec 1, 2025):**
- Orders placed before **18:00** → must be shipped same working day
- Orders placed after **18:00** → must be shipped by **11:59** the next working day

Source: Dec 2025 + May 2026 updates

## Shipping Fees (Forward)

From September 3, 2025:

- **Failed-Delivery Orders:** Forward shipping fees settled by sellers (Standard orders: capped at 40,000₫; Instant delivery: capped at 20,000₫)
- **Customer Returns (including "No Longer Needed"):** Forward shipping fees settled by sellers after deducting platform shipping vouchers

**Exceptions (seller NOT charged):**
- Partial Return Orders (buyer returns only part of order)
- Refund-Only Orders (no physical return)
- "Shipped by Seller" Orders

Source: Aug 2025 update

## Refund Timelines

| Scenario | Auto-approved from Jan 26, 2026 |
|----------|---------------------------------|
| Product deactivated by platform | Refund-only request auto-approved |
| Shop closed due to policy violations | Refund-only request auto-approved |

**Seller-to-Customer Returns (from May 2026):** After dispute resolution, seller may be
asked to return item to buyer within 3 business days. Economy shipping fully reimbursed.

## Cash-on-Delivery (COD) — VN-Specific

From January 18, 2026 (test phase):
- Eligible COD orders via J&T in selected provinces: buyers may inspect outer packaging before payment
- Orders over 500,000 VND and Fresh & Frozen Food are excluded
- For multi-item deliveries: accept or reject the entire order
- Province list: An Giang, Bac Lieu, Ben Tre, Ca Mau, Can Tho, Dong Thap, Hau Giang, Kien Giang, Long An, Soc Trang, Tien Giang, Tra Vinh, Vinh Long

`REGION-VARIANT: VN` — COD unboxing policy is VN-specific and in testing phase.

Source: Jan 2026 update
