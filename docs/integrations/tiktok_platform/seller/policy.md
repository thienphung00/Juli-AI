### Seller Fulfilment Performance Standards

| Metric | Violation | VP Issued |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Seller-Fault Cancellation Rate (SFCR) | High SFCR | 2–4 VP |
| Late Dispatch Rate (LDR) | High LDR | 2–4 VP |
| Seller-Fault Return/Refund Rate | High rate | 2 VP |
| SNAD (Significantly Not As Described) | Shipping SNAD items | 1–48 VP |

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=10015235&lang=en

### Customer Service Requirements

| Metric | Violation | VP |
|--------|-----------|-----|
| 24-hour response rate | Low rate | 1 VP |
| Customer communications | Inappropriate / prohibited content | 1–12 VP |

Good customer service enables:
- Eligibility for Star Seller / Mall programs
- Campaign and Flash Sale eligibility
- Store Rating improvement

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7199855642370&lang=en

### Qualification Requirements

| Qualification issue | VP Range |
|-------------------|---------|
| Missing qualifications | 6–48 VP |
| Expired qualifications | 4–48 VP |
| Inauthentic qualifications | 12–48 VP |

Sellers must maintain valid, up-to-date documentation for their registered product categories.

### Content Rules

| Violation | VP |
|-----------|-----|
| Reproduced/duplicated content | 48 VP |
| Prohibited/unsupported product promotion | 6–48 VP |
| Inconsistent product promotion | 1–48 VP |
| Activities prohibited by law | 48 VP |

**Joint penalties:** If a seller's linked creator account violates platform policies, TikTok Shop
may apply joint enforcement against the seller's shop.
