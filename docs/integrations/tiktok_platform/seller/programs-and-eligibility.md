## Star Shop+ Program

Upgraded tier of Star Shop, introduced December 3, 2025 (free trial at launch).

### Eligibility

| Requirement | Threshold |
|-------------|-----------|
| Star Shop status | Maintained for past 3 consecutive months |
| Store Rating subscores | Product Satisfaction, Fulfillment & Logistics, Customer Service all ≥ 3.5 |
| Voucher Extra Program (VXP) | Participation is **mandatory** |

Eligibility reviewed automatically each month.

### Benefits

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Advertising coupons | 50% discount, up to 150 million VND |
| OHC Tool Premium | Free access — increase daily order limit up to 5×, +2 working days fulfillment extension for extra orders |
| Express settlement | 1 calendar day settlement (with Store Rating ≥ 4.5) |

Source: Nov/Dec 2025 update

## TikTok Shop Mall

Curated virtual shopping mall for verified brands, reputable retailers, and authorized resellers.

### Benefits

- TikTok Shop Mall badge + badge of authenticity on shop and all products
- Higher visibility in customer shopping journey
- Access to dedicated Mall campaigns and benefits programs
- Express Settlement (1 day with Store Rating ≥ 4.5)
- OHC Tool Premium from June 8, 2026

### Eligibility (criteria updated Feb 2025)

`UNKNOWN` — exact current criteria not fully published in reviewed source. See Seller Center
and TikTok Shop Academy Mall article.

**Key notes:**
- Mall sellers: Standard settlement is 15 days (Extended) unless they qualify for Express (1 day)
  — as of June 9, 2026 (formerly 15-day default for Mall)
- Mall sellers can no longer use Proof of Purchase for Brand Qualification applications (from Mar 12, 2025)

Source: Jan 2025, Feb 2025 updates

## Affiliate Marketing Program (Seller side)

Allows sellers to recruit creators to promote their products via the TikTok Shop Affiliate system.

### Shop-level eligibility

| Requirement | Threshold | Since |
|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Store Rating | ≥ 3 | Sep 2024 |
| Counterfeit VP | None in last 60 days | Sep 2024 |
| Service-related NRR violations | None | Apr 2026 |
| Serious complaint volume | No high volume of empty-parcel/counterfeit/fraud complaints | Oct 2025 |

### Product-level eligibility (SFRR thresholds)

| Category | Max SFRR (products with ≥5 seller-fault returns) |
|----------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Electronics | < 3% |
| Lifestyle & Fashion | < 2.8% |
| FMCG | < 2.5% |

### Suspension on serious complaints

A shop or product may be restricted from Affiliate Collaborations 14–30 days (depending
on severity) for serious customer complaints including empty parcels, counterfeit goods,
or suspected fraud.

### Perishable fruits gate (VN-specific, from Apr 20, 2026)

Highly perishable fruits (avocado, citrus, durian, longan, lychee, rambutan) require:
- Store Rating ≥ 3 AND Product Satisfaction Score ≥ 3

`REGION-VARIANT: VN`

Source: Sep 2024, Oct 2025, Mar-Apr 2026 updates
