## 1. Affiliate Center (Seller → Creator Collab)

### 1.1 Collaboration Modes

TikTok Shop provides sellers two primary collaboration structures for recruiting and managing
creator affiliates:

| Mode | Visibility | Commission | Creator selection | Conversion rate |
|------|-----------|-----------|------------------|----------------|
| Open Collaboration | Public — any eligible creator can join | Fixed rate set by seller (typically 10–15%) | Passive; creators self-select | 2–4% (broad reach) |
| Targeted Collaboration | Private — invite-only | Negotiable (typically 18–50%) | Active recruitment | 8–12% (creator-product fit) |

Source (non-official synthesis): https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/home?role=seller&menu=feature  
Data from: https://www.hamstergarage.com/article/tiktok-shop-affiliate-program-guide-rates-fees  
**Note:** Specific rate ranges above are from community sources, not official TikTok docs.
Treat as `UNVERIFIED` for precise thresholds.

**Commission priority:** Targeted > Open. If a creator is in a Target plan, that rate applies;
otherwise Open plan rate is used.

### 1.2 Commission Rate Management (Seller-Side)

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Rate increase | Takes effect immediately |
| Rate decrease | **30-day grace period** for creators who already added product to showcase or linked in videos |
| Commission formula (creator receives) | `(Revenue − Refunds) × Commission rate` |
| Target commission (invite) | Negotiable, not visible to other creators |
| Open commission | Fixed, visible in Product Marketplace to all eligible creators |

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=7904880287155985&lang=en

### 1.3 Creator Discovery & Recruitment (Seller-Side)

| Tool | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Find Creators | Browse creators by category, follower count, engagement metrics |
| Manage Creators | Track active affiliates, commissions, performance |
| Affiliate Analytics | View affiliate-attributed GMV, conversion, creator performance |
| Samples management | Track free sample requests from Affiliate Creators |
| Campaign Auto-Registration | Automatically enroll eligible products into platform campaigns |
| Open Collaboration listings | Product Marketplace listing for passive creator discovery |

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/home?role=seller&menu=feature (nav — articles not retrieved)

### 1.4 Enrollment Gates (Seller)

Sellers lose access to affiliate enrollment when they breach certain policy thresholds:

| Event | Restriction duration |
|-------|---------------------|
| 12 Violation Points | Cannot enroll in TikTok Shop Affiliates Program for 7 days |
| 24 Violation Points | Cannot enroll in TikTok Shop Affiliates Program for 14 days |
| 36 Violation Points | Shop deactivated for 28 days (full access lost) |

Source: https://seller-vn.tiktok.com/university/essay?knowledge_id=10015960&lang=en
